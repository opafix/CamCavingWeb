How to Join

So you want to go caving?

Fantastic news! We are always keen for new members.

The best way to join us is to come and meet us at the pub on a Tuesday evening. Alternatively, you can send an email to one of the committee or send a message to our facebook page. The About Meets page contains most of the information about the main caving weekends. Synchronise your calendar with ours on our Calendar Page so you don't miss any events.

We're looking forward to seeing you!
