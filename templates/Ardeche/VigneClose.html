Aven de la Vigne Close

How to find it

From parking for Aven Reynaud, follow road until GR4 track is seen heading off to the left (red/white stripe). Turn in; parking shortly afterwards on left. Follow track to first junction; take left fork. Some distance on (*) there is an obvious track on the left, marked high up with a sign with "Vigne Close" in feint writing. Take the track; a small fork goes off on the right, but follow the main track to suddenly arrive at the hole in a copse.
