Grotte de Bunis

How to find it

Follow track past the parking point for Grotte du Barry to reach a parking space on the right just before a left-hand bend. Follow the path from the back of the parking space (a little overgrown in places) to reach a large entrance, with earth slope leading down, which is slightly concealed in the trees on the right of the track.
