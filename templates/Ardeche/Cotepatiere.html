Grotte de la Cotepatiere

How to get there

Near Saint-Paul-Le-Jeune, drive out the back of Chadouillet down a track over a bridge to reach the parking spot, walk along dry river bed.

Equipment

no gear required

Description

Horizontal cave with stemples at entrance, boats near end.
