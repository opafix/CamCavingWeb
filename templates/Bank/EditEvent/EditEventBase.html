Tip - To register the accounts for a meet, you should:

  1. Select all club members as creditors
  2. Select all relevant pools as debtors
  3. Users will now appear as rows, and Pools as columns. Each cell represents the credit on the User from the Pool. A negative value signifies a debit

For instance, if Joe Bloggs were to have been on a meet costing £35.00, he would be credited -35.00 from the Meets Pool, signifying a debit on his account of £35.00 and a credit to the Meets Pool of £35.00.

Similarly, if John Smith were to have been on a meet and spent £40.00 on petrol, he would be credited 40.00 from the Meets Pool, signifying a credit on his account of £40.00 and a debit to the Meets Pool of £40.00.

(This is the basic format of double-entry account keeping)
