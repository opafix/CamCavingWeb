Training Meets

In order to prepare the club for SRT (Single Rope Technique) underground, we hold regular training sessions. In the winter months, we use the FitzPatrick Hall in Queen's College. It has an industrial rigging frame at the top and is ideal for allowing closely supervised learning of SRT.

As well as basic ascending and descending technique, we practice rescue techniques such as hauling rigs and mid-rope rescue.
