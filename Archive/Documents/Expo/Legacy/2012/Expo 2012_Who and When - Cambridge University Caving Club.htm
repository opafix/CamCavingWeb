Notes:
I assume that drivers and passengers can agree on mutually convenient picking up arrangements. Please contact your driver/passenger before Monday 16/07 and let me know only if agreement can't be reached.
If you aren't on one of the two legs of the journey I have you down as independently travelling that leg of the journey (this is the case for Olaf, Jenny + Olly, etc...). Contact me if this isn't the case and/or modify the plan to put yourself down in one of the cars.
