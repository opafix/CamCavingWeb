Stuff Left at Top Camp

5 250g bags of massed potato, ½ Box of mashed potato, 80 Darren drum of plain Oatso, 20 Flavoured Oatso, 30 Assorted soups new this year, 20 Tomato Soup, 12 Packs egg noodles, 30 Personal noodles, 1 bag of muesli, 3 Packs of dried apricots, 1 Pack of dried fruit, 200 Tea bags, 1kg sugar, ½ Darren drum of custard,

3 Raumer hammers, 1 Orange handle hammer, 3 Raumer spit drivers (Rubber handle), 4 Lightweight spit driver, 3 Hilti setters, 1 pot 10 spits, 3 hiltis, 1 pot 8 hiltis, 1 pot 10 spits, 10 hiltis, 10 Empty pots, some with lids attached by string, 1 pot cones for spits? 4 measuring tapes, Fork Handles, 1 capping mat rubber 6” sq, 3 13mm spanner, 1 First aid kit, first response peli case, 1 Large first aid kit, 1 Lump hammer, 2 Crow bars, 1 Chisel, 2 New 10mm drills hilti, 1 New 10mm Draper bit, 1 Cheap 10mm drill bit, 2 1.5V Lithium batteries, 2 Large batteries, 3 Large solar cells, 4 Smaller solar cells, 1 Solar controller 20A Perspex, 10m Deviation cord, 2 500mm 8mm studding, 1 700mm 8mm studding for stoves, 1 Buffalo sleep bag, Details of battery, Small battery, Yuassa NPC38-12I, Large battery, 350mm, 168mm, 180mm high.

Next year we could do with the two large drums from base camp taking up, there is some stuff in the grey boxes, basically it was a little tight getting it all in rodent proof damp proof containers.



Expo attic list

In the Ammunition box 19 Clowns, 43 Ring hangers, 23 Mailioins, 29 Hangers with Maillions, 4 Arse hangers, 37 Bends and twists without maillions, 10 Troll Bollards, 5 Hilti drill bits, used but still sharp, 1 13mm spanner.


6 Tuna twists self-heat cans that didn't get hot when they were in date, but are now a fair few years past their sell by date, they're really heavy as well, which is fine for taking down the cave but crap if you have to take them out, especially when they didn't get hot and they ming when cold, people swear by them, I swear at em <end rant>.

Baked beans and tins of veg, general base camp food, Tomato purée stuff, Cutlery, scourers and sponges, Drum with Rice, Instant peppers, Salt, Fair few instant soup catering packs quite old but nice,

Drum of Bog roll, Top camp meals that are unlikely to be eaten until we run out of all the other food. 10 Tins Morn-flake, unless you like this stuff it's mainly useful as cement, even the mice don't eat is when dried on...

Drum with Duncan's Radio, why does Duncan's radio always go in a drum, it dosen't fit and the drum would be much more use up at top camp where it is needed, if it still seals, (Note Duncan's radio needs a converter between 3.5mm male jack and stereo phono line male (red n white), preferably 3-5m, the 2m one we have is too short and limits location of computer to always being in the way.

Bread making stuff (no flour you might need this, get the stuff from penny mark with yeast added works).

1 Box loo rolls, egg noodles.

1 box of survey stuff inc. nail varnish.

1 Box 10 slabs flapjacks, 4 boxes of 10 shitters of oatso, I wouldn't bank on this if the mice find it...

1 Ream of paper.

Half box of Persil.

Bolts and odds and sods in Makita box.

New printer cartridge.

Reel thin blue string.

3 red boxes of really nasty cheese sauce mix, smells like used walking socks, probably considered a delicacy in Chelsea.

1 red box of curry sauce mix.

Box of 12 bottles of sweet chilli sauce, makes top camp food top.

2 boxes of egg noodles (2009), loads.

1 box cheese sauce mix (5 off 2” tubs), noodles, 1 jar peanut butter, lots instant mash, oatso simple (1 box of 10 sachets).

1 large drum containing, More egg Noodles, two bags of sugar, bags of instant mash, pasta, the nice muesli, (Didn't get to count stuff in drum, but it's a big drum)

Box – plain oatso Box – rice, custard, pasta Lots and lots of other assorted stuff including the tables and benches, the store is a little full.

We need to reduce the amount of random crap stored next year, and that doesn't mean throwing all the un-claimed cutlery away.

There's lots of un-stackable partially damaged boxes, we need to get a load of the 25l frying lard drums from Hilda, wash them out and put all the food in those (rodent proof and stackable)
