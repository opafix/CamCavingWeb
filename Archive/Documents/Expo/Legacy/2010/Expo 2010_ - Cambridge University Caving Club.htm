Expo 2010

POST-Expo 2010 - survey of Tunnockschact uploaded by Becka [[1]]

This section of the wiki is just a tool to help get expo 2010 preparation done in a coordinated fashion.

Note that the bulk of information about expo is at [2], including pages on every expo cave, expo year, and an expedition handbook.

Once the tasks and discussions below become more concrete they will be transferred to the main Expo website so that the expo website remains up to date.

  • Expo 2010 mission statement

We are currently discussing ideas for this years expedition. Emphasis will be on Tunnockschacht, Steinbrueckenhoehle and Kaninchenhoehle.

  • Expo committee has to the formed
  • Signup form will follow in late spring

The provisional Expo dates are 24 July - 29 August (5 weeks). The "who and when" is now live.
