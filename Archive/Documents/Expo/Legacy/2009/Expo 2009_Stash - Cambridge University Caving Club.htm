Other ideas

These are a few other ideas that people have emailed to me, they are not as drawings, but if you like the idea feel free to draw it and send it to me.

1) London Underground style map - Cambridge Underground, made up of major caves, connections and entrances in our area of Austria

  • Something similar was done in 1997 Duncan Collis
  • I've seen this too, DUSA and others. --Ian W 14:10, 31 May 2009 (UTC)

2) Map, including real caving surveys from Austria, leading to above-ground cartoon sketch of top and base-camp, people drinking beer at base camp, far in the distance landmarks such as the Cambridge pub, and the road with a random vehicle packed like a tower or a historic car-crash on the way to expo. all annotated.

2) The cycle of evolution from monkeys on branches through cavemen to cavers with anything in between - different kind of hominins slowly getting off the branches, standing up, then crouching to get themselves dangling on ropes.

  • Didn't either DUSA or Carduff UCC do this a year or two ago? Edvin
  • A popular caving theme, incl DUSA 2007. --Ian W 14:10, 31 May 2009 (UTC)
  • This has been done to death by lots of different groups. I don't know why it suddenly (recently) became popular; it's a very old idea.

3) Timeline of inventions: fire, stone tools, cave art, pottery, metalworking, writing, gunpowder, tea, linux, ponys, SRT kits.

  • Or major discoveries made by CUCC in the area. MCG did this for the back of a T-shirt, rock-band-tour style. --MikeTA 16:59, 31 May 2009 (UTC)

4) Seeing as a major theme of this years Expo is a return to KH, how about something along these lines? (Back down the rabbit hole)

Final design

We are going to go with the nerdy design (windows, linux n mac expo) in large on the back, and the CUCC in mailons as a front logo, brest print.
